Survival Essentials

A successful survival world doesnt happen on the first try! It takes a few shots and attempts to be able to jump in and get it right off the bat. Thats why the block exchange has taken the liberty of making a quick intro guide based on thi spicture to let you know how to go from coal to dimonds in no time! Take a look at the picture above.

Here we have what looks like a generic image, but in fact it's a great guide to a starting house! As you can see location is key, this user set camp at a small lake or pond which is a stratigic move on their part. This provides a player with an early start on some crucial elements like fishing for food. Additionally it helps the player with a super important survival basic, a farm. In the picture we see a small wheat and sugar cane farm providing the player with atleast five pieces of bread giving a steady amount over time to provide for regeneration. Another part of the location is the forest alowing for easy access to wood, one of the most important resources in the game. Also outside the house we see various animals. These guys are super useful if accessable due to thier buffs to your attack, speed, and protection. Lastly we see the house, for a early game house this is great, the most important thing is that it has the essentials, crafting table, chest, furnace, a bed. At the begining of a new world the size of your house dosn't necessairly matter as long as you have what you need too survive.
